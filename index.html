About WiTG

Why WittyGirlsClub?

STEM should be shaped by everyone. WiTG creates a supportive and kind space for women and non-binary folks in Goa to learn, build, and grow together.

Learn

Hands-on workshops, talks, and study groups covering topics in Software, Research, AI, Engineering, Mathematics, Entrepreneurship, Diversity & Inclusion, Sustainable Tech and others.

Connect

Build a network with like minded professionals, build your portfolio, find mentors, land opportunities, and shape your career right here in Goa.

Grow

Level up your skills, take on new challenges, and grow your career with a community that has your back.

Why This Space Is Needed

The research is clear. Communities like WiTG are not just nice to have, they are essential.
