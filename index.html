About WitG

Why WittyGirls?

Technology should be shaped by everyone. WitG creates a space for women in Goa to learn, build, and grow — together.

Learn

Hands-on workshops, talks, and study groups — from web development to AI, ethics, and sustainable tech.

Connect

Meet like-minded women across Goa — developers, designers, founders, students, and career-changers — all welcome.

Grow

Build your portfolio, find mentors, land opportunities, and level up your career — right here in Goa.
