Our Purpose

Why Women in Tech Goa?

We believe technology should be shaped by everyone. Our community creates opportunities for women to thrive in Goa's growing tech scene.

Learn

Hands-on workshops, talks, and study groups covering everything from web development to AI and sustainable tech.

Connect

Meet like-minded women across Goa — developers, designers, founders, students, and career-changers — all welcome.

Grow

Build your portfolio, find mentors, land opportunities, and level up your career in tech — right here in Goa.
