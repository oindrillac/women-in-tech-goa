## ✨ What is WiTG?

WiTG (aka WittyGirls) is a community for women and non-binary folks in STEM across Goa. Whether you're a software engineer, scientist, data analyst, hardware tinkerer, mathematician, researcher, student, or someone exploring a career switch into STEM, this is your space!

Technology and science should be shaped by everyone. But the reality is stark: it takes over 100 years to close the global gender gap at the current pace (World Economic Forum), India's tech industry faces a massive women retention crisis (NASSCOM), and the first promotion to manager is where women fall behind and the gap only widens (McKinsey).

WiTG exists because dedicated, safe spaces for women in STEM are not just nice to have, they are essential. We're building a community where you can learn, connect, and grow without being the only woman in the room. 💪

## 🎯 What We Do

📚 **Learn** - Hands-on workshops, talks, and study groups covering Software, Research, AI, Engineering, Mathematics, Entrepreneurship, Diversity & Inclusion, and Sustainable Tech

🤝 **Connect** - Build a network with like-minded professionals, find mentors, land opportunities, and shape your career right here in Goa

🚀 **Grow** - Level up your skills, take on new challenges, and grow your career with a community that has your back
